What do we do at Afghan Charity Week?

In 2019, university societies came together to launch Afghan Charity Week (ACW), driven by a shared commitment to give back to those in need in Afghanistan. As students, we aim to make a meaningful difference through collective action and compassion.

  • ➤ To establish a strong community among the Afghan student diaspora, creating a network of societies that will support and share resources with one another.
  • ➤ To nominate a registered charity annually, ensuring funds are collected and distributed responsibly on behalf of Afghan Charity Week, with complete impartiality.
  • ➤ To always remain inclusive and build sustainable proceeds to ensure longevity.
  • ➤ To encourage charitable work among Afghan university societies across the UK and internationally.
